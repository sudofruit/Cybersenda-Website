  • Services

Description

Our project faced the challenge of conducting a professional eyewear photoshoot on a tight budget. With limited resources, including an entry-level Nikon DSLR and no studio lighting, we navigated the obstacles of a startup environment. Additionally, the reflective surfaces of the eyewear presented a significant hurdle, requiring us to find creative solutions to reduce glare and improve the overall image quality.

These limitations pushed our team to innovate, leading to the creation of a cost-effective DIY light box and a deep understanding of refraction and reflection principles. Despite these challenges, our team approached the project with determination, transforming constraints into opportunities for resourcefulness and creativity.

Description

When we undertook an e-commerce photography project for Rospetals, a renowned pet bottle manufacturer, our main goal was to captivate the audience with the wide range of their products. The challenge was to showcase each pet bottle's unique features and create a visually stunning presentation.

We carefully adjusted the size of each product and strategically minimized the reflective surface using studio lighting during the photoshoot. To add a professional touch and achieve optimal lighting, we incorporated both hexagonal and square softboxes. This combination enhanced the visual appeal of every pet bottle. Our commitment to perfection extended to post-processing in Photoshop, where we flawlessly reduced any remaining reflective surfaces, resulting in a polished final product. The client was thrilled with the outcome and expressed their appreciation for the impactful visual representation of their brand.
